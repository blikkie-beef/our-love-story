The most beautiful woman this world has ever known.

And who is she to him, you ask? I'll tell you.

Faith is a woman of unparalleled depth—her soul, though tortured by life’s many trials, shines with an inner strength that defies the darkness she's faced. She carries the weight of sorrow and pain, bearing scars that tell stories of resilience and survival. Through it all, she remains gentle, her heart tender, even after the world has tried to harden it.

Behind her soft smile lies the strength of someone who has endured more than most will ever know. Yet, despite her past, she continues to love fiercely, to give selflessly, and to live with a grace that only those who have been tested by fire can possess.

She is both fragile and unbreakable—a paradox that makes her so uniquely beautiful.

Her tortured soul does not define her but fuels her ability to find joy in even the smallest moments. She is a reminder that even the most hurtful experiences cannot dim the light within someone who refuses to give up. To me, Princess Baby is more than just the love of my life—she is a symbol of hope, of what it means to rise from the ashes stronger than before.

And just who is he?

He is the lucky one. The one who got away.

For as long as he can remember, he has walked this world alone, racked with sorrow, burdened by secrets too dark to speak. He grew up in isolation, hiding the unimaginable things he had done, living a life that no one could truly know or understand—until he met her, his Princess Baby.

She was the first to see beyond the mask he wore. With her gentle heart, she pulled the truth from him, the truth he had buried beneath layers of pain, shame, and lies. She is the only person who fully knows him—the darkness he carries, the nightmares that haunt him, the memories of watching his best friend die, of losing so many others along the way. She knows the degenerate he was and loves me anyway. He's a lucky man.

What does it feel like?

"Someone like you", is exactly what's been missing from my life for the entirety of it." - Princess Baby, 07 June 2024

She is more than his love—she is his salvation. Together, they are two broken souls who have found solace in one another. She holds him when the darkness closes in, and he does the same for her. In her arms, he has found peace. In her heart, He has found a reason to keep fighting, to keep living. A Promise of things we have to do, baby.

Now that the 3 of us have each other, it's us vs. the world. And only we remain in control of our future. As such, we have to fulfil these promises to each other:

A Promise of Growth

As husband and wife, as a family. We're starting from small beginnings, but together we'll build a family unit so strong, we'll give to our children, and to each other, what we never had. A happy, healthy family, loving on each other, having fun, keeping each other safe, and living life on our terms, in our little bubble.

A Promise of Communication

In this journey we share, let us always promise to keep our hearts open and our words flowing. No matter the storms we face, we will communicate with honesty and love, nurturing the bond that unites us. We will listen with understanding and speak with kindness, ensuring that our voices remain intertwined, even in the silence.

A Promise of Adventure

Together, we promise to embrace the spirit of adventure, to keep the flame of our romance alive with every new experience we share. Let us explore the world hand in hand, seeking out the beauty in every corner, whether it’s a spontaneous trip or a quiet moment together in nature.

A Promise to The Jason

Together, we promise to be the best parents we can be for our beloved son, Jason. We will nurture him with love, patience, and understanding, creating a safe and supportive environment where he can thrive. Our goal is to provide him with a life filled with opportunities, joy, and the freedom to explore his dreams.

A Promise of Expression

Together, we promise to explore the depths of our creativity as a means of expressing ourselves—you through the written word, and me through the melody of music. Through writing and music, we will find healing and understanding, transforming our pain into art that resonates with our souls. Together, let us embrace the power of our creativity as a way to connect, communicate, and deepen our bond.

A Promise of Loyalty

Together, we promise unwavering loyalty to one another, standing as an unbreakable team against the challenges life may throw our way. Nothing and no one will ever come between us; our bond is forged in trust and love, creating an unstoppable force that can weather any storm.
